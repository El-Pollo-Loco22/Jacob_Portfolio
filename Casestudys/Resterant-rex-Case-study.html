Overview

Restaurant Rex: AI-Powered Restaurant Discovery

Restaurant Rex is an innovative AI-powered platform that revolutionizes how people discover and choose restaurants. By combining natural language processing with a comprehensive restaurant database, it creates personalized dining recommendations through engaging conversations. The project aimed to solve the common challenge of decision paralysis when choosing where to eat by providing contextual, intelligent suggestions based on user preferences, mood, and circumstances.

The Challenge

Reimagining Restaurant Discovery

The traditional approach to finding restaurants often involves scrolling through endless lists, reading numerous reviews, and still feeling uncertain about the choice. Restaurant Rex aimed to solve this by creating a more natural, conversation-based discovery process.

User Research Insights

Key Findings:
• Users spend 20+ minutes on average choosing restaurants
• 73% feel overwhelmed by too many options
• 89% value personalized recommendations
• 65% prefer conversation over traditional search
• 92% want real-time availability information

Design Solution

Conversational Intelligence

The solution centered on creating an AI-powered conversational interface that could understand context, remember preferences, and make intelligent recommendations while maintaining a natural, friendly dialogue with users.
