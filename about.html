My story
I'm Jacob, a Visual designer from Bed-Stuy, Brooklyn. I am a multi media swiss army knife I have a strong foundation in graphic design, motion graphics, Video editing, and visual effects. This is where I discovered my love for visual storytelling and expression through color and images. My Journey naturally led me to UX design, where I can combine my passion for crafting engaging experiences with the problem-solving mindset I’ve built over the years. New York's vibrant mix of cultures and experiences has always been a source of inspiration for me. It’s a constant reminder that great design should be inclusive and cater to diverse needs. My background in visual effects and motion design allows me to create visuals that grab attention like city lights at night. Just as clear signage directs you through a crowded city street, my skills in graphic and user-centered design ensure users find their way seamlessly.
This diverse toolkit that I’ve developed over the years allows me to craft user journeys that are not just functional, but also engaging and memorable.
